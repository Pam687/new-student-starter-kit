Making Friends

Starting a new school can feel scary, but there are lots of ways to meet people.

Talk in Class

  • Say hello in chat when class starts
  • Participate in discussions
  • Be respectful and friendly

Join Clubs

  • Look for clubs that match your interests
  • Attend regularly
  • Introduce yourself to other members

Group Work

  • Collaborate with classmates
  • Share ideas
  • Support each other
