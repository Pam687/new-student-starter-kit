How Online School Works

Online school can feel different at first, but once you learn the system it becomes easy.

Joining Classes

  • Check your timetable every morning
  • Join lessons on time
  • Make sure your microphone and camera work

Submitting Homework

  • Complete assignments before the deadline
  • Upload work to the correct class
  • Ask your teacher if you are unsure

Staying Organised

  • Keep a digital or paper planner
  • Save your files in subject folders
  • Review notes after each lesson
